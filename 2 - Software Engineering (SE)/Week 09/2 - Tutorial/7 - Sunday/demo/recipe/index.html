Grandma's Secret Apple Pie

This apple pie recipe has been in my family for generations!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 6 medium-sized apples, peeled and sliced

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. In a bowl, mix sugar and cinnamon
  3. Add the apples in the sugar-cinnamon mix
